About Me

My name is Patton Parham and I have resided in Nashville for 8+ years now. I have my Bachelor's of Science in Audio Engineering from Belmont University. That is what prompted me to move to Nashville, TN in the first place. I work in this field along with running my own hand-crafted jewelry business under A1Obsidian. Having been out of school for three years with my Audio background, I have recently made the decision to pursue computer coding with prospects of audio application development.
